About Us

ROBO-TRONIX is FSAC’s student robotics club, where creativity meets technology. We specialize in designing and building electronic and robotic systems, teaching coding, AI integration, and mechanical engineering. Our projects span from autonomous robots to smart IoT devices, helping students gain hands-on experience and prepare for future engineering careers.

Our mission is to nurture curiosity, problem-solving, and teamwork. Members engage in workshops, mentorship programs, and competitions at national and international levels. ROBO-TRONIX is more than a club — it is a hub for innovation, knowledge sharing, and technological excellence.

Our Projects

At ROBO-TRONIX, our projects cover diverse areas of robotics and electronics. Students work on robotic arms that demonstrate precise motion and automation capabilities. We design drones for aerial navigation and obstacle detection, and autonomous vehicles for AI-driven transport experiments. Our IoT initiatives include smart home automation systems, sensors, and data acquisition devices.

Each project focuses on practical problem-solving, innovation, and team collaboration. Members gain experience in programming languages like Python, Arduino, and C++, learn mechanical design principles, and develop critical thinking skills. Competitions and real-world challenges prepare our students to excel in engineering and technological careers.

Join Us

Becoming a member of ROBO-TRONIX is your gateway to learning, creating, and innovating in the field of robotics. Students participate in hands-on workshops, receive mentorship from faculty, and collaborate on exciting projects. By joining, you not only enhance your technical skills but also join a community of passionate innovators shaping the future of technology.

Membership is open to all FSAC students with a curiosity for engineering and robotics. Whether you are experienced or a beginner, ROBO-TRONIX provides the environment, tools, and guidance to turn your ideas into real-world solutions.
